My Hobbies in Detail

Reading

I'm an avid reader with a particular interest in science fiction and fantasy novels. Some of my favorite authors include Isaac Asimov, J.R.R. Tolkien, and Ursula K. Le Guin. I typically read for at least 30 minutes every day, which helps me relax and expand my imagination.

Recently, I've been exploring non-fiction works about technology and psychology, which complement my academic studies in computer science.

Photography

Photography allows me to capture and share my perspective of the world. I enjoy landscape and street photography, focusing on composition, lighting, and storytelling through images.

I use a DSLR camera for most of my photography, but I also experiment with smartphone photography, proving that great photos can be taken with any device with the right technique.

Sports

Basketball is my primary sport, which I've been playing since high school. I enjoy both the physical exercise and the strategic aspects of the game. I play with friends weekly and follow professional basketball leagues.

Besides basketball, I also enjoy swimming and hiking, which allow me to stay active while enjoying nature.
